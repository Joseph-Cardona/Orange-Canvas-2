Guidelines

Treat Others with Respect

Show respect and kindness to all individuals, regardless of their background, opinions, or abilities.

Avoid any form of verbal, physical, or emotional harassment or abuse.


No Politics

Use of figures related to politics and flags are prohibited.


Keep It Kid Friendly

Swears, inappropriate drawings, and anything not safe for young children are banned.


Respect Laws

Observe all applicable laws, rules, and regulations, both local and international.


Obey Trademarks And Copyrights

Do not use any trademarked or copyrighted properties in your drawings.


Don't Hack

Refrain from abusing any of the website's APIs and features.


Use Common Sense

Use your common sense; don't try to find loopholes.


No Spam or Self-Promotion

Prohibit users from spamming the website with repetitive or irrelevant content.

Avoid self-promotion, advertising, or excessive links to external websites without proper authorization.


Respect Others' Feedback and Opinions

Encourage users to provide constructive criticism and feedback, but emphasize the importance of delivering it in a respectful and supportive manner.

Remind users not to take feedback personally and to stay open to different perspectives.


Proper Use of Reporting Feature

Explain the appropriate use of the reporting feature for reporting offensive or inappropriate content.

Discourage false reporting or misuse of the reporting system.


No Impersonation

Prohibit users from impersonating others or creating fake accounts.


Respect Site Administrators and Moderators

Emphasize the authority of site administrators and moderators.

Instruct users to follow their directions and decisions regarding content and behavior.


Data Security

Ensure users understand the website's data security measures and their responsibilities in safeguarding their personal information.


No Plagiarism or Art Theft

Explicitly state that users must not copy or steal others' artwork and claim it as their own.

Encourage proper attribution and credit for any referenced or collaborative work.


Age Verification and Parental Consent

If applicable, implement age verification for users to access certain features.

Require parental consent for users under a certain age to use the website.


Clear Consequences for Violations

Specify the consequences of violating the guidelines, including warnings, temporary bans, or permanent account suspension.


Update Policy

Mention that the guidelines may be updated from time to time, and it's the user's responsibility to stay informed about any changes.
